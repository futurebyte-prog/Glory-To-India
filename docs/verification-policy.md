# 📜 Verification Policy

"History deserves evidence."

Glory to India is committed to preserving India's history with accuracy, transparency, and respect for diverse historical sources.

This document defines how historical information is reviewed, verified, and published.

---

# 🎯 Our Principles

## Every contribution should strive to be:

- Accurate
- Transparent
- Respectful
- Evidence-based
- Open to correction

The objective is not to prove a narrative, but to preserve history responsibly.

---

# 📚 Accepted Sources
The following sources are considered acceptable when documenting historical information.

## Primary Sources

Highest priority should be given to:

- Government archives
- Historical manuscripts
- Court records
- Official documents
- Museum records
- Archaeological reports
- Contemporary letters and diaries

---

## Secondary Sources

Examples include:

- History books
- Academic publications
- Peer-reviewed journals
- University publications
- Research papers

---

## Community Sources

The following may be accepted with appropriate labeling:

- Oral traditions
- Interviews
- Family records
- Local historians
- Village documentation
- Community archives

These sources are valuable but may require additional verification.

---

## ❌ Unacceptable Sources

The following should not be used as the sole evidence for historical claims:

- Unsourced social media posts
- AI-generated content presented as evidence
- Anonymous blogs without references
- Forwarded messages
- Rumors
- Unverified screenshots

AI tools may assist with writing or translation but cannot replace historical sources.

---

# 🏷️ Verification Levels

Every article receives one of the following statuses.

## ✅ Verified

Requirements:

- Supported by reliable historical evidence.
- References are available.
- Reviewed by project maintainers.

---

## 🟡 Partially Verified

Requirements:

- Some claims supported.
- Additional verification required.
- Article remains publicly visible with appropriate labeling.

---

## 🟠 Folk Tradition

Used when information is based primarily on:

- Oral history
- Community memory
- Cultural traditions

These stories are valuable and preserved, but readers should understand they may not have documentary evidence.

---

## 🔴 Needs Review

Applied when:

- Sources are missing.
- Claims are disputed.
- Additional research is required.

Such content should not be treated as established historical fact.

---

# 👨‍⚖️ Review Process

## Reviewers should evaluate:

- Source quality
- Historical consistency
- Citation completeness
- Neutral language
- Internal consistency
- Duplicate submissions

Whenever possible, multiple independent sources should support important historical claims.

---

# ✍️ Citation Guidelines

Every historical claim should include citations whenever available.

## Preferred citation information includes:

- Author
- Title
- Publisher
- Publication Year
- Page Number (if applicable)
- Archive or Museum Reference
- DOI or ISBN (if available)

---

# 🌍 Neutrality

Glory to India is committed to documenting history responsibly.

## Contributors should:

- Present evidence fairly.
- Distinguish facts from interpretation.
- Clearly identify disputed claims.
- Avoid inflammatory or insulting language.
- Respect differing scholarly viewpoints when supported by credible sources.

---

# 🤖 AI Policy

## Artificial Intelligence may be used for:

- Grammar correction
- Translation
- Formatting
- Summarization

AI must not be treated as a historical source.

Contributors remain responsible for the accuracy of every submission.

---

# 🔄 Corrections

History is an evolving field.

## If new evidence becomes available, articles may be:

- Updated
- Corrected
- Reclassified
- Expanded

Previous revisions should remain accessible through version history whenever possible.

---

# 🤝 Community Responsibility

Every contributor shares responsibility for maintaining the quality of this archive.

# If you discover an error:

- Open an issue.
- Provide supporting evidence.
- Suggest corrections respectfully.

Constructive discussion is encouraged.

---

# ❤️ Our Commitment

Glory to India exists to preserve India's historical and cultural heritage for future generations.

We believe that preservation without accuracy is incomplete, and accuracy without transparency is difficult to trust.

Our goal is to build an archive that values evidence, welcomes community participation, and remains open to continual improvement.
