# 🎯 Long-Term Vision

Glory to India aims to become a trusted, open-source platform for preserving India's historical and cultural heritage.

## The project will focus on:

- Historical accuracy
- Source transparency
- Community collaboration
- Open knowledge
- Long-term preservation

---

# 📅 Development Philosophy

The project is developed with a quality-first approach.

Rather than releasing features quickly, the goal is to build a reliable and trustworthy platform that can serve future generations.
